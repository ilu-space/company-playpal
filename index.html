How it works

From signup to playdate
in four simple steps

PlayPal does the heavy lifting — you just show up, relax, and enjoy watching your kids make new friends.

1

Create your family profile

Set up profiles for each of your kids — ages, interests, personality — so matching actually means something.

2

Get smart matches

Our algorithm surfaces compatible families nearby, filtered by age, availability, and what your kids love to do.

3

Connect & chat

Like a profile to show interest. When the feeling's mutual, a chat opens so you can get to know each other first.

4

Schedule & go

Propose times, pick a spot, confirm — done. PlayPal adds it to your calendar and sends reminders automatically.
